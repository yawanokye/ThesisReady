Core features

Built for real academic project work

The platform adapts to the student’s level, chapter format, research approach and evidence supplied.

Chapter Builder

Drafts Chapter One to Chapter Five from selected sections and school-specific requirements.

Research Source Finder

Searches open scholarly metadata sources and attaches relevant papers before drafting.

Literature Review Assistant

Supports conceptual, theoretical, empirical, objective-based, thematic and econometric reviews.

Methodology Builder

Adapts to primary data, secondary data, qualitative, mixed methods and econometric studies.

Results Upload

Uses uploaded Excel, CSV, Word, PDF or text results to support Chapter Four drafting.

Included revision policy

Every paid chapter or article includes one revision

The included revision covers structure, flow, supervisor comments, citation strengthening, methodology justification, tense correction and DOCX readiness. It does not cover changing to a completely new topic, rewriting a different chapter, replacing the entire methodology or unlimited supervisor-review rounds.

Additional revision: 40% of the original chapter or article price.

Responsible academic support

Designed to support students, not replace academic judgement

ProjectReady AI helps students organise ideas, draft chapters, interpret supplied results and improve academic structure. Students remain responsible for verifying originality, sources, data, results, citations, supervisor instructions and final submission requirements.
