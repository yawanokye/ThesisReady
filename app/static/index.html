Core features

Built for real academic project work

The platform adapts to the student’s level, chapter format, research approach and evidence supplied.

Guided Chapter Development

Develops editable working drafts for Chapters One to Five from user-selected sections, supplied research materials and school-specific requirements.

Research Source Finder

Searches open scholarly metadata sources and attaches relevant papers before working-draft development.

Literature Review Assistant

Supports conceptual, theoretical, empirical, objective-based, thematic and econometric reviews.

Methodology Builder

Adapts to primary data, secondary data, qualitative, mixed methods and econometric studies.

Results Upload

Uses uploaded Excel, CSV, Word, PDF or text results to support Chapter Four working-draft development.

Compliance Review

Checks drafts against selected guideline requirements and exports a DOCX checklist report.

Chapter Strengthener

Revises an existing chapter at the selected academic level, deepens citations and discussion, addresses supervisor comments, and exports changes in blue with action items in red.
