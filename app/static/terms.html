Terms of use

ProjectReady AI guided research-support terms

By using ProjectReady AI, the user agrees to use the platform as a self-service research-development, revision and compliance tool for authorised academic work.

Working-draft status: Platform outputs are editable AI-assisted working materials. They are not guaranteed to be complete, accurate, original, institutionally compliant or ready for submission.

User authorship and contribution

The user must provide and control the research topic, objectives or questions, study context, sources or evidence, methodological decisions, and actual data or results where applicable. The user remains responsible for authorship, verification, critical revision, ethical compliance and final submission decisions.

Prohibited activities

  • Using the platform to ghostwrite or complete academic work for another person.
  • Buying, selling or submitting completed theses, dissertations, assignments or examinations.
  • Fabricating data, results, references, quotations, ethics approvals or institutional facts.
  • Misrepresenting authorship or bypassing institutional academic-integrity requirements.
  • Using generated material without independent review and source verification.

Service scope

Anovlad Technologies provides software access and automated research-support functions. It does not assign human writers, promise academic acceptance, provide supervisor approval or submit material to an institution.

Payments and chapter access

Each paid chapter plan applies to the project and chapter identified during checkout. The plan includes only the stated working-draft, strengthening, compliance and export entitlements and expires according to the displayed access period. A different project or chapter requires separate access unless the plan states otherwise.

Source, data and result verification

Metadata search results, citations, interpretations and suggested methods must be checked against original sources and institutional requirements. The platform must not be treated as the final authority on facts, statistics, methodology, law, ethics or academic policy.

Privacy and confidential academic material

Users should avoid uploading information they are not authorised to process. Where research data or supervisor material contains confidential or personal information, the user must remove or protect it as required by applicable institutional and legal rules.

Changes and availability

Features, prices, models and access rules may change. Temporary service interruptions may occur. These terms should be read together with the Academic Integrity and Responsible Use Policy.
