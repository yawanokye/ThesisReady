Academic integrity and responsible use

ProjectReady AI supports the researcher's own academic work.

ProjectReady AI is a self-service software platform for research planning, guided working-draft development, chapter strengthening, evidence organisation, source support, results integration, formatting and compliance review. It is not a managed writing service.

Output status: All generated or revised material is an editable AI-assisted working draft. It requires independent source verification, factual checking, critical revision, ethical review and appropriate supervisor or institutional approval before submission.

What users must provide

  • Their own research topic, problem context, objectives or questions.
  • Institutional or supervisor requirements.
  • Relevant sources, evidence, notes and academic judgement.
  • Methodological decisions and, where applicable, actual data, results or findings.
  • Independent review, verification, revision and final authorship responsibility.

Permitted support

  • Organising research ideas and chapter structures.
  • Developing editable working drafts from user-supplied inputs.
  • Strengthening the user's existing writing and responding to supervisor comments.
  • Finding scholarly metadata, checking relevance and flagging missing verification.
  • Integrating user-supplied results without inventing findings.
  • Reviewing selected guideline and compliance requirements.

Prohibited use

  • Ghostwriting or completing academic work on behalf of another person.
  • Selling, purchasing or submitting completed theses, dissertations, assignments or examinations.
  • Impersonating an author or misrepresenting authorship.
  • Fabricating data, results, quotations, ethics approvals, institutional facts or references.
  • Submitting generated material without review, verification and appropriate acknowledgement or permission required by the user's institution.
  • Using the platform to evade institutional academic-integrity processes.

Anovlad Technologies' role

Anovlad Technologies does not assign staff or third-party writers to complete academic work for users. It does not sell prewritten academic work and does not submit work to institutions. Users remain responsible for authorship, accuracy, originality, lawful and ethical use, and final submission decisions.
